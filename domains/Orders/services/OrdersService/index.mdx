## Overview

The Orders Service manages key workflows for customer orders throughout their lifecycle—from placement and validation to amendments, cancellation, and confirmation. Enhanced validation and handling improvements now ensure that order events (confirmation, amendment, cancellation) consistently reflect updated business logic, including proper setting of status and reasons, with minor code refactoring for reliability.
