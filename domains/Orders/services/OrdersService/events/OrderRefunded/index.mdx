## Overview

The `OrderRefunded` event is triggered whenever an order has been refunded, either fully or partially. This event communicates to downstream finance, customer notification, and reporting services that a refund has occurred, including the reason and the refunded amount. Consumers should use this event to initiate payment reconciliations, notification flows, or analytical updates without being coupled to other order-cancellation logic.
