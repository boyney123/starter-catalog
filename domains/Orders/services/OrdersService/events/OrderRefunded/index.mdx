The OrderRefunded event is triggered whenever a customer initiates a refund for their order. This event ensures that all relevant services are notified of the refund, allowing them to take appropriate actions such as updating inventory, processing financial transactions, and notifying the user. It helps maintain consistency across the system when order statuses change due to refunds.
