## Properties

- **orderId**: The unique identifier for the order.
- **userId**: The unique identifier for the user associated with the order.
- **orderStatus**: Status of the order after the refund.
- **totalAmount**: Total amount of the order that was refunded.
- **refundAmount**: The specific amount that was refunded.
- **refundReason**: Reason for the refund.
- **refundedAt**: Timestamp of the refund.
